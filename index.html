Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data when using our tools.

Our Privacy Commitment

At SignatureTool.in, we believe that your privacy is a fundamental right, especially when it comes to personal identification documents. This privacy policy explains our strong commitment to keeping your data secure and private.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos, signatures, or any personal data.

All processing happens entirely in your browser with no data ever leaving your device.

How Our Tools Work

Understanding how our tools work is essential to understanding our privacy practices:

  1. Local Processing: When you use our photo and signature combiner, all image processing happens directly in your web browser using HTML5 Canvas technology.
  2. No Server Communication: Unlike many similar tools, our application never sends your images to any server. There is no API call, no upload, and no data transmission of any kind.
  3. Zero Data Collection: We do not collect your IP address, browser information, or any usage data related to your specific image processing.
  4. Temporary Memory: Images are temporarily stored in your browser's memory only for the duration of your session and are automatically cleared when you close the page.
  5. No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your usage of our tools.

This approach ensures that your sensitive personal information remains completely under your control at all times.

What Data We Do Collect

To operate our website and improve our services, we collect limited anonymous information that cannot be used to identify you:

  • Aggregate Usage Data: We collect anonymous information about how many people use our tools and which features are most popular. This data is completely aggregated and cannot be traced back to any individual user.
  • Technical Information: Basic information about your browser type and device dimensions to ensure our tools work properly across different devices.

None of this information can be used to identify you personally or determine what specific images you processed with our tools.

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to operate our website:

  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to ensure consistent typography. Google may collect information as described in their privacy policy.
  • Font Awesome: We use Font Awesome for icons. Their privacy practices are described in their privacy policy.

Importantly, none of these services have access to the images you process with our tools, as those images never leave your browser.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal information about your tool usage, there is no personal data for you to access, correct, or delete. However, you have complete control over:

  • The images you upload to our tools
  • Your browser's cache and temporary storage

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us using the information on our Contact page.
